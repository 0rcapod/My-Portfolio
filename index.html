About Me

Something about me and what I do...

No shortcuts

My name is Dion. I am a full time flight Dispatcher and an aspiring web developer.

I've been in the aviation industry for more than 8 years and having worked in 3 different companies, I realized how programming helped us in our daily tasks. Not only did it make it easier but it also structured, organized and documented our work. I got curious and wanted to learn more.

During the pandemic, I took the time to research more about programming and eventually I took that first step. I applied to an online course and it was whole lot of learning from there.

Lesson after lesson and project after project, my knowledge grew. I am far from being an expert and I know I won't get there soon but I'm continuously learning and working very hard.

The map for Web Development is very broad and I'm just getting started...
